Is your air conditioner blowing weak or smelling musty? Regular filter rinsing isn't enough. Our Professional AC Deep Cleaning service is designed to eliminate 99% of bacteria, mold, and dust that accumulate inside your cooling system over time.

Why Deep Cleaning is Essential

An uncleaned air conditioner is a breeding ground for pathogens. When you breathe in air from a dirty unit, you risk respiratory issues and allergies. Beyond health, dust buildup on the evaporator coils forces the compressor to work harder, increasing your electricity bills by up to 30% and shortening the lifespan of your equipment.

Our Step-by-Step Process

  • Full Disassembly: We remove the outer casing to reach hidden components.
  • Antimicrobial Treatment: Application of specialized, eco-friendly cleaning agents to kill mold.
  • High-Pressure Wash: Using professional tools to flush out debris from the drainage system and coils.
  • Fan Blade Sanitization: Cleaning the blower wheel to restore maximum airflow.
  • Final Testing: We check the air output temperature and humidity levels.

Don't wait for a breakdown. Experts recommend a deep clean every 6 months for residential units and every 3 months for offices. Book your cleaning today and breathe fresh, healthy air again!
