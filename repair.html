Expert AC Repair Services

A broken air conditioner during a heatwave is more than an inconvenience—it's an emergency. At AC-Master, we provide fast, reliable AC repair services for all major brands, including Daikin, Mitsubishi, LG, and Samsung.

Common AC Problems We Fix

Our certified technicians carry a full inventory of spare parts to resolve most issues on the first visit. We specialize in:

  • Compressor Failure: Replacing or repairing the "heart" of your AC.
  • Refrigerant Leaks: Finding microscopic cracks and performing a precise Freon refill.
  • Electrical Issues: Fixing faulty capacitors, contactors, and control boards.
  • Drainage Clogs: Solving water leakage problems instantly.
  • Strange Noises: Diagnosing rattling sounds from the fan or motor.
